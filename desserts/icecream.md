Easy No-Churn Vanilla Ice Cream
Ingredients
* 2 cups heavy whipping cream (cold)
* 14 oz sweetened condensed milk (chilled)
* 1 tbsp vanilla extract or paste
Instructions
Whip: In a large bowl, beat heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
Fold: Gently stir in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla until smooth.
Freeze: Pour into a container, cover tightly, and freeze for 6+ hours
